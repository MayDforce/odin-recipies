Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Soft and fluffy cinnamon rolls with a sweet cinnamon filling and a drizzle of icing. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a sweet treat any time of day.

Ingredients

  • Pre-made dough or homemade dough
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Brown sugar
  • Powdered sugar (for icing)
  • Milk (for icing)

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough into a rectangle and spread with softened butter.
  2. Sprinkle a mixture of cinnamon and brown sugar evenly over the dough.
  3. Roll the dough tightly into a log and cut into individual rolls.
  4. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish and let rise for 30 minutes.
  5. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20–25 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Drizzle with icing made from powdered sugar and milk before serving.
