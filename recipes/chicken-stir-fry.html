Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe

A quick and healthy chicken stir-fry with fresh vegetables and a flavorful sauce. Perfect for a weeknight dinner that’s ready in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast, sliced
  • Bell peppers, sliced
  • Broccoli florets
  • Carrots, sliced
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic and ginger, minced
  • Olive oil or sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken slices and cook until no longer pink.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5–7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Pour in soy sauce and toss to coat everything evenly.
  5. Cook for another 2 minutes to let the flavors combine.
  6. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
