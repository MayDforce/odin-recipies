Traditional Lasagna recipe

Traditional lasagna is one of the most iconic dishes in Italian cuisine. Layers of fresh pasta combined with rich meat ragù, creamy béchamel sauce, and plenty of cheese make this a true comfort food. It’s perfect for family gatherings and Sunday meals.

List of ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Meat ragù (beef and pork)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat ragù by cooking ground beef and pork with tomato, onion, and olive oil.
  2. Spread a thin layer of ragù on the bottom of a baking dish.
  3. Add a layer of pasta, followed by ragù, béchamel, and cheese.
  4. Repeat the layers until the dish is full.
  5. Finish with plenty of Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for about 30–40 minutes, until golden on top.
